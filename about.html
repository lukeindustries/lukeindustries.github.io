Luke Industries is a design studio that specialises in brand, identity and logo design.

Based in Cork, Ireland and led by Luke Slattery, our work spans design, strategy and production encompassing brands and identities, products and packaging, websites and digital experiences.

Luke Industries has a collaborative, problem-solving approach to design, with personal involvement by Luke Slattery in every project and continuous attention to the details and nuances of projects as they evolve.
